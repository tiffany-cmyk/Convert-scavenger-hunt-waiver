Waiver & release

Activity description

The Scavenger Hunt ("Activity") is a team-based event held throughout the designated venue at Experimentation Elite: Space Academy on 7 May 2026, sponsored by Convert.com ("Convert"). Participants will navigate indoor and outdoor areas of the event space, complete challenges, interact with installations and props, collaborate with other attendees, and may be photographed, filmed, or recorded as part of the event experience.

Scope of activity

Participation in this Activity may involve:

  • Physical movement — walking, navigating stairs, crouching, and moving through varied event spaces;
  • Team challenges — completing puzzles, tasks, and interactive stations throughout the venue;
  • Photography & recording — you may be captured in photos, videos, or live streams produced by Convert, Experimentation Elite organisers, or other attendees;
  • Data collection — submission of your name, email, and participation data for event administration, communications, and Convert's legitimate business interests. Convert handles all personal data in accordance with applicable data protection law.

Assumption of risk

I acknowledge that participation in the Activity involves inherent risks, including but not limited to: physical injury from movement through the event space (slipping, tripping, falling, collision); exertion-related discomfort, particularly for those with pre-existing medical conditions; and minor property damage incidental to challenge activities. I voluntarily and knowingly assume all such risks.

Release of liability

In consideration of being permitted to participate, I — on behalf of myself, my heirs, executors, and assigns — release, waive, and discharge Convert.com, its officers, employees, agents, partners, and venue providers from any and all claims, demands, actions, or causes of action arising out of or related to any injury, damage, or loss I may sustain during the Activity, including those caused by the ordinary negligence of the released parties.

Media & image consent

I grant Convert.com irrevocable permission to use photographs, video recordings, and other media captured during the Activity for promotional, editorial, and marketing purposes, including on Convert's website and social media channels, without compensation and without further notice.

Data use consent

I consent to Convert collecting, storing, and processing my personal information submitted via this form for the purposes of event administration, participant safety, and Convert's marketing communications. I understand I may withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time. Convert will not sell my personal data to third parties.

Participant conduct

I agree to follow all instructions from Convert and event staff, remain within designated activity zones, cease participation immediately if I feel unsafe or unwell, and report any hazards or incidents to a staff member promptly.

Governing law

This waiver shall be governed by the laws of the jurisdiction in which the event is held. If any provision is held unenforceable, the remaining provisions continue in full force.
